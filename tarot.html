Patti is a native New Yorker and spiritual practitioner who has been reading Tarot for more than 20 years. As a Scorpio Sun, Scorpio Moon, Virgo Rising, Patti's strong intuitive skills, coupled with her desire to be of service to others, make her uniquely qualified to help clients gain clarity, perspective, and direction in their lives.

During her sessions, Patti may pair Numerology or Charm Casting with Tarot to add a fun and insightful element to a reading. She also offers Chakra Tarot readings that help balance the chakras and clear them of any blockages.
